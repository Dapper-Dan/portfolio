My Story

I've always had a fascination with the workings behind every day things, the mysteries of science and technology. As a child I would take apart VCRs, appliances, anything I could get my hands and see what made them tick. As a teenager, I was building modified gaming consoles out of the parts from other consoles. As an adult, I studied mechanical engineering at California State Univeristy Chico. After college, I started traveling and exploring the Earth's mysteries. I worked within the travel industry around the world and settled down in San Francisco, California where I managed an international hostel for 4 years. My path eventually led me to the stage craft industry where I collaborated with various companies and teams to build festivals, concerts, and large scale technology conventions. After a long time of attending conventions by Google, Apple, and other giants and seeing all of these developers being in the culture I loved and having my dream profession, I took a leap and applied to a top-tier web development bootcamp. I have since graduated and continue to build new applications and expand my arsenal of skills.
